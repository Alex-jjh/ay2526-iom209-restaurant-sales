# Key References from Deep Research Report

## 最值得加入 Proof of Reference 的文献（按优先级排序）

### Tier 1: 必须引用（直接支撑核心论点）

**1. Al-Marzouki et al. (2005) — 数据造假的统计检测**
- Al-Marzouki, S., Evans, S., Marshall, T. and Roberts, I. (2005) 'Are these data real? Statistical methods for the detection of data fabrication in clinical trials', *BMJ*, 331(7511), pp. 267–270. doi:10.1136/bmj.331.7511.267.
- Link: https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC1181267/
- 用途: 支撑"convergent null results = evidence of fabrication"的核心论点
- 截图要点: "statistical features so strongly suggestive of data fabrication that no other explanation is likely"

**2. Andreyeva et al. (2010) — 食品价格弹性（已有，补充数据）**
- 已在 Ref 1，但 deep research 提供了更精确的数据: "food away from home elasticity 0.7-0.8"
- Link: https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC2804646/

**3. Bernhard et al. (2015) — 温度对食物摄入的量化影响**
- Bernhard, M.C., Li, P., Allison, D.B. and Gohlke, J.M. (2015) 'Warm ambient temperature decreases food intake in a simulated office setting: A pilot randomized controlled trial', *Frontiers in Nutrition*, 2, p. 20. doi:10.3389/fnut.2015.00020.
- Link: https://www.frontiersin.org/journals/nutrition/articles/10.3389/fnut.2015.00020/full
- 用途: 量化温度效应 — 每升高1°C，摄入减少85.9 kcal (p=0.002)
- 截图要点: "for every 1°C increase in peripheral temperature, participants consumed 85.9 kcal less"

**4. Pope et al. (2014) — 新年决心效应的量化**
- Pope, L. and Hanks, A.S. (2014) 'Healthy food purchases increase 29.4% after New Year', *PLOS ONE*, 9(12), e110561. doi:10.1371/journal.pone.0110561.
- Link: https://journals.plos.org/plosone/article?id=10.1371/journal.pone.0110561
- 用途: 真实数据中 January healthy food +29.4%，对比你们的 p=0.556
- 截图要点: "healthy food purchases increased 29.4% ($13.24/week) after the New Year"

**5. Blanke et al. (2022) — 意图-行为差距（解释 H7 反向结果）**
- Blanke, E.S., Brose, A., Kalokerinos, E.K., Erbas, Y., Riediger, M. and Kuppens, P. (2022) 'Intention-behavior gap in healthy and sustainable food shopping', *Frontiers in Nutrition*, 9, 742614. doi:10.3389/fnut.2022.742614.
- Link: https://journal.frontiersin.org/article/10.3389/fnut.2022.742614
- 用途: 解释为什么 Google Trends ↑ 但实际购买 ↓ (Cohen's d=1.78)
- 截图要点: "large gaps between stated intentions and actual behavior for sustainable food (Cohen's d=1.78)"

### Tier 2: 推荐引用（加强论证深度）

**6. Cohen (1992) — 统计功效分析**
- Cohen, J. (1992) 'A power primer', *Psychological Bulletin*, 112(1), pp. 155–159. doi:10.1037/0033-2909.112.1.155.
- 用途: 支撑 n=24 功效不足的论点

**7. Lakens (2017) — 等价性检验 TOST**
- Lakens, D. (2017) 'Equivalence tests: A practical primer for t tests, correlations, and meta-analyses', *Social Psychological and Personality Science*, 8(4), pp. 355–362. doi:10.1177/1948550617697177.
- Link: https://journals.sagepub.com/doi/10.1177/1948550617697177
- 用途: 将 null results 重新框架为 "effects equivalent to zero"

**8. Stelmach-Mardas et al. (2016) — 季节性饮食变化 meta-analysis**
- Stelmach-Mardas, M., Rodacki, T., Dobrowolska-Iwanek, J., Brzozowska, A., Walkowiak, J., Wojtanowska-Krosniak, A., Zagrodzki, P., Bechthold, A., Mardas, M. and Boeing, H. (2016) 'Seasonality of food groups and total energy intake: A systematic review and meta-analysis', *European Journal of Clinical Nutrition*, 70(6), pp. 700–708. doi:10.1038/ejcn.2015.224.
- Link: https://www.researchgate.net/publication/290432999
- 用途: 26 项研究的 meta-analysis 确认冬季能量摄入显著更高

**9. Hill et al. (2012) — 口红效应原始论文**
- Hill, S.E., Rodeheffer, C.D., Griskevicius, V., Durante, K. and White, A.E. (2012) 'Boosting beauty in an economic decline: Mating, spending, and the lipstick effect', *Journal of Personality and Social Psychology*, 103(2), pp. 275–291. doi:10.1037/a0028657.
- 用途: 支撑 "foodluxxing" / 经济衰退中的小奢侈理论

**10. Ackoff (1989) — DIKW 金字塔原始论文**
- Ackoff, R.L. (1989) 'From data to wisdom', *Journal of Applied Systems Analysis*, 16(1), pp. 3–9.
- 用途: BI 框架的理论基础
